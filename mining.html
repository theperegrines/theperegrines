Mining Industry

It's no secret that the world’s largest mining companies are investing immense resources in finding technological solutions to increase efficiency, improve safety and streamline operations. Drones are becoming an integral tool in this transformation, replacing manual inspections in the most hazardous areas and collecting critical information, while drastically reducing the time and manpower required.Planning, managing and ensuring safety throughout the mining life-cycle.

Planning

Effective mine planning is of paramount importance for the success of operations. Drone solutions offer visual information that ensure robust mine planning.

What is the problem?

Cross-department co-ordination is limited by the restricted use and unreliable nature of information. Due to logistical blindspot we can't be identified most economical and safe route to transfer material and due to this we are facing mine collapse issues, loss of materials and men power due to the wrong route.

How do we solve it?

The Peregrines Drone Solutions develope an automatic,AI-based on-demand solution for collecting aerial data in mining facilities without the logistics involved in our drone operations. The on-demand solution provides a direct connection to aerial data and new levels of insight. This autonomous drone, streamlines operations, increases efficiency and offers immediate, easy access to the required data.

Operations

Drone solutions offer a smarter way to make mining operations more safe, economical and accountable.

What is the problem?

A mine presents "logistical blindspots" which means the most economical and safe routes for material movement are not identified. Collapse of mine walls leads to increased downtime, loss of equipment and men.

How do we solve it?

Reduce the mismatch between projected and actual production Greater insight to integrate planning with scheduling activities.

Closure

The indirect environmental cost of mining activities can be staggering. Drone solutions offer a vital insight to plan effective reclamation and rehabilitation activities.

What is the problem?

Only observer estimations and categorical assessment of erosion and potential pollution creates "reliability blindspots".

How do we solve it?

By delivering outputs that intuitively represent and quantitatively validate degrees of erosion and extent of afforestation and rehabilitation activities drone solutions ensure that accountability is extended till the very end of the mining cycle.

What we offer?

Inspection

Mapping

Provide mapping information for centralized database initiatives such as NLRMP and Online Mine Tenement Registry System.

Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of mine operations to track changes in stockpile volume and mine topography.
